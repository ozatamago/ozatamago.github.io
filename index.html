Yusuke Ozaki

Research interests: Bayesian Optimization, Active Learning, Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), AI for Science / Lab Automation, Single-cell omics & GRN inference

I am Yusuke Ozaki, an undergraduate in Computer Science at Kwansei Gakuin University and an exchange student in the Department of Computer Science at the University at Albany, SUNY. My interests lie at the intersection of Bayesian optimization, graph neural networks, active learning, and lab-automation–oriented machine learning. I aim to contribute to robust, data-efficient machine learning systems and tools, including optimization engines, recommender systems, and agent-based platforms for real-world applications.

Hobbies: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, reading books.
